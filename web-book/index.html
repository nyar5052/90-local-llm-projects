❌ Monthly API costs ($50-500+)
❌ Rate limits & downtime
❌ Vendor lock-in risk

Why Local LLMs Matter

🔒

Complete Privacy

Your data never leaves your machine. No third-party servers, no data collection, no privacy concerns. Perfect for sensitive business and personal data.

💰

Zero API Costs

No monthly subscriptions, no per-token billing, no surprise charges. Once set up, run unlimited queries at zero marginal cost.

⚡

Speed & Reliability

No network latency, no rate limiting, no outages. Your AI assistant is always available, even without internet connectivity.

🛠

Full Control

Customize models, fine-tune behavior, and adapt to your specific needs. No vendor lock-in, complete freedom over your AI stack.
